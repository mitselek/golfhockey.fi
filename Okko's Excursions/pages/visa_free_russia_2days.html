SEE RUSSIA VISA FREE
2-DAY DOOR-TO-DOOR TOUR

Beautiful twoday trip to Russian Vyborg, a lively border town, which belonged to Finland prior to 2nd World War. Tour goes via Lappeenranta which is a lakeside town 200km North-East of Helsinki. Trip to Russia nd back is made with cruise-boat Carelia through the Saimaa Canal and Lake Saimaa. Boat has a restaurant, bar, tax free shop and sundeck. Major sights enroute are guided in Finnish and English. Time just flies while watching the beautiful sights enroute.

No visa is required. We only need your passport-copy 3 working-days prior the tour.

All guest must have a valid passport with them on this tour.

Note. If you dislike early departure look for tour with overnight in Lappeenranta.
