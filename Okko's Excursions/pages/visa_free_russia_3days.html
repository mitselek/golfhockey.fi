SEE RUSSIA VISA FREE
3-DAY DOOR-TO-DOOR TOUR

Beautiful three day trip to Russian Vyborg, a lively border town, which belonged to Finland prior 2nd World war. Tour goes via Lappeenranta which is a lakeside town 200km North-East of Helsinki. Trip to Russia and back is made with cruise-boat Carelia through the Saimaa Canal and Lake Saimaa. Boat has a restaurant, bar, taxfree shop and sundeck. Major sights enroute are guided in Finnish and English. Three full days time in Vyborg to experience, feel and taste Russia. Possibility to make a fullday excursion or a do-it-yourself commuter-train trip to St.Petersburg which is just a 2 hour ride from Vyborg. Ask more.

No visa is required. We only need your passport-copy 3 working-days prior the tour.

All guest must have a valid passport with them on this tour.

Note. If you dislike early departure look for tour with overnight in Lappeenranta.
