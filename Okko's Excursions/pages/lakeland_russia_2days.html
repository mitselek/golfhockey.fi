FINNISH LAKELAND + VISA FREE RUSSIA
2-DAY DOOR-TO-DOOR TOUR

This two day tour combines one of the most beautiful Finnish lakeside towns and Russian Vyborg. You spend one night in Lappeenranta before and after your fullday trip to Russia. Lively Russian border town Vyborg belonged to Finland prior 2nd World War. Ride to Russia is made with a comfortable tourist-bus and return to Finland with cruise-boat Carelia through the Saimaa Canal and Lake Saimaa.

No visa is required. We only need your passport-copy 3 working-days prior the tour.

All guest must have a valid passport with them on this tour.
